# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in the Piveo community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Showing empathy and kindness toward others
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback
- Taking responsibility and learning from mistakes
- Respecting differing opinions and experiences

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language
- Trolling, insulting comments, and personal attacks
- Publishing private information without explicit permission
- Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct and may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this policy.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is officially representing the project.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainers via the channel documented in `SECURITY.md`.
